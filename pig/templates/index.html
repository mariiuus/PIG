What Is PIG?

PIG is a service that allows you to partition people into groups. PIG lets you define what parameters you want to divide by, and lets the users answer the parameters from a predefined scale.

Why Use PIG?

PIG makes it easy to divide your students into groups as optimally as possible. This means that you need not use valuable time dividing into well-fitted groups, PIG does it for you!

How Do You Use PIG?

After registering, this is the functionality for the types of users:

Professor
  • Create a division - Add as many parameters as you want. When you are finished, click "Create Division".
  • View your divisions - Find links to distribute to your students and teacher assistant. View division info.
  • View division info - Here you can see the groups for your division, students withou a group, and run the group division.
Teaching Assistant
  • Receive a link from your professor to sign up for a division as a Teacher Assistant.
  • View your groups - Here you can view the groups your are a teacher assistant for.
Student
  • Receive a link from your professor to sign up for a division as a Student.
  • View your groups - Here you can view the groups you have been placed in for all divisions.
